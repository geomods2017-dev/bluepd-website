Everything You Need, Right in Your Pocket

Designed by and for law enforcement — the tools that matter most, always at your fingertips.

📜

Miranda Warnings

Full Miranda rights text available instantly. Never second-guess wording in the field — accurate, legally current, and always accessible.

⚖️

Updated Case Law

Browse landmark and recent case law relevant to patrol and investigations. Stay current on rulings that directly impact your work.

State Criminal Codes

Search and reference state statutes for your jurisdiction. Quickly confirm elements of offenses during stops, arrests, and report writing.

Evidence Capture

Capture, organize, and securely store photos and notes tied to incidents directly within the app. No more scattered camera rolls.

Standardized Field Sobriety Test guidance and checklists to ensure proper documentation during DUI investigations.

Protected access ensures sensitive reference materials and saved reports stay private. Your data stays with you.

Quick Reference Cards

Fast-access cards for the information you reach for most — organized for speed when every second counts.

Saved Reports

Save and review past SFST reports and field notes directly within the app for follow-up, court prep, or supervisor review.
